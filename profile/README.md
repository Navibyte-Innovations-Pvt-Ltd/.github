## 👋 About Us

**NaviByte Innovations** is a Pune-based product engineering company delivering **custom software, web platforms, mobile apps, and cloud solutions** to businesses across industries.

Founded in **2024** by a team of experienced software engineers and technology entrepreneurs, we are proudly **incubated at the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) Research Park Foundation** and winners of the **SPPU Startup Olympiad** — a recognition of our innovation, engineering excellence, and entrepreneurial drive.

We have shipped **50+ production projects** — from e-commerce storefronts to enterprise-grade SaaS — helping founders, SMBs, and enterprises achieve digital transformation with modern, reliable technology.

## 💡 How We Work

- **Product-first engineering** — every line of code ties to a user outcome.
- **Small teams, short loops** — decisions land the same day, not the same quarter.
- **Type-safe end-to-end** — from database schema to UI prop, one source of truth.
- **AI-native tooling** — we use LLMs where they earn their keep, skip them where they don't.
- **Ship early, refine in production** — with telemetry, feature flags, and real users.
